Hey, I'M Hadid Awaludin.
I have more than 8 years of experience as a Creative Branding Specialist, Visual Designer, SEO Content Writer, and Creative Campaign Strategist.

I help brands and businesses build stronger digital identities through strategic branding, effective visual communication, SEO-driven content, and result-oriented creative campaigns. My approach combines creativity, branding strategy, and storytelling to create engaging digital experiences that are relevant to today’s audience and market needs.

Throughout my professional journey, I have completed more than 827 tasks and projects in the electrical cable manufacturing industry, while also handling more than 53 contracts and project collaborations with various companies in Indonesia outside of my primary role. I am also one of the founders of Itico Tech & Agency, focusing on creative solutions, digital branding, and visual communication development.

These experiences have strengthened my skills in problem solving, project management, creative execution, and understanding business and visual communication needs across various industries.

I am also an active contributor with more than 928 published licenses on Adobe Stock, Magnific, and Shutterstock, continuously sharpening my visual skills, exploring creative concepts, and developing design approaches that align with current digital market trends.

Open to collaboration opportunities, freelance projects, and professional connections in branding, creative strategy, digital content, and visual communication.
